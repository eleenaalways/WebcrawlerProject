UNSW is located on the unceded territory of theBedegal(Kensington campus), Gadigal (City and Paddington Campuses) and Ngunnawal peoples (UNSW Canberra) who are the Traditional Owners of the lands where each campus of UNSW is situated.

Explore campus your way

Take a 360 tour of our beautiful campus and get to know the buildings, study spaces and cafes that will become your second home.

Our Kensington campus is up and running for 2022, with the campus open to visit to the public. Feel free to check out our facilities and buildings to get a sense of the UNSW lifestyle.

Learn about our state-of-the-art facilities

Design Futures Lab

Part of the Faculty of Arts, Design and Architecture this lab aims to inspire exploration, innovation and research into fabrication, emerging technologies, and design theories.

Engineering Makerspaces

Our three Makerspaces provide high quality hands-on experience for all members of the UNSW community.

MCIC Makerspace

The Michael Crouch Innovation Centre (MCIC) specialises in prototyping for start-up ideas and entrepreneurial activities.

The Making Centre

The Making Centre brings together the many workshops located at the Paddington campus faculty of Arts, Design and Architecture.
