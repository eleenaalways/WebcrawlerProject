I've submitted my application. What's next?

  • After you have submitted your application, you can easily track its progress via yourApply Online account. You can also upload any additional documents required.Once you've provided all the necessary documentation, it should take two to three weeks for UNSW to assess your application.

  • We'll notify you of the outcome of your application via email. If your application is successful, you'll receive a full offer or a conditional offer if you need to complete more steps. If you're receiving assistance with your application, your nominated agent will also receive a copy of the email.

  • If you receive a full offer, you'll also receive an email with a link to your personalised offer page. Your page will guide you through the process of accepting or deferring your offer. For international students, once you have accepted and paid your deposit, you'll receive an electronic confirmation of enrolment (eCoE).

  • Onceenrolment for your degree is available, you can enrol in your degree and courses online atmyUNSW.
