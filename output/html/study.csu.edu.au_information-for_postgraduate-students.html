    Online study that fits around you

    Upskill with Australia's most experienced online university. Everything you'll need is available at the click of a mouse: lectures, tutorials, assessments, resources and support. IT help comes as standard, and you can access free software, including Microsoft Office 365, as well as our full digital library. Need books sent to you? We’ll post them free of charge.

    Learn more about online study

    Support

    When you undertake postgraduate study with us, support is just a click away. We have online learning advisers available every day until 7pm – via phone, email, video call or online chat. Talk to our study skills team about everything from researching topics to writing and referencing assignments. And you can connect with the health and wellbeing team whenever you need.
