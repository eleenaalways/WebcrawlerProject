      Upskill, retrain or get started with our wide range of TAFE certificates and diplomas available online and on campus at locations across regional Queensland.

      Choose from entry-level Certificate I and II courses for a basic introduction or gain more advanced knowledge and skills and be job-ready with a Certificate III or IV. Our TAFE Diplomas are the next step up, with in-depth study that can lead to more highly skilled job outcomes and pathways to further study.

      Apprenticeships and Traineeships

      Our apprenticeships and traineeships are developed in consultation with industry and involve practical learning experiences.

      School-Based Apprenticeship and Traineeship (SAT)

      Find out more information about completing a School-Based Apprenticeship or Traineeship with CQUniversity.

      Short Courses and Micro-credentials

      Build your skills and enhance your employability with our range of in-demand short courses and micro-credentials.

      TAFE Subsidised Training

      Reduce the fees you pay with government subsidised training, from free to low-fee TAFE course options.
