Actuarial science and data analytics

Find meaning in the numbers

For those who dont just want to find a pattern, but find meaning in it actuarial science or data analytics might be for you.

With a focus on financial risk and data analytics, Bonds programs combine elements of economics, finance, statistics, data science, and advanced mathematics to develop techniques for risk management and business decision-making.

Our innovative and immersive programs produce graduates who are well-placed to enter the job market, with highly transferrable skills that can transcend industries and countries. This approach can lead to a range of roles in diverse areas such as climatology, e-commerce, energy and environment, health, public infrastructure, marketing as well as the more traditional insurance and financial sectors.

At a glance

  • Bond is in the top 2 per cent of business schools in the world, accredited by both AACSB and EQUIS.   

  • 82.3 per cent of graduates from our undergraduate programs are in full-time employment just four months after graduation.
