Accommodation

Everything is on the doorstep when you live on campus – you’re at the heart of things!

We have accommodation on all six campuses, offering a great mix of country and city living in a safe and supported environment.

Want to live on campus in 2026? Applications are open!

Three reasons living on campus is great

1

Guaranteed room for most first year students

Most first-year students who apply are guaranteed a room on campus.

To be eligible for our first year accommodation guarantee if you're a domestic student, you'll need to complete your accommodation application before Friday 31 October 2025.

If you’re an international, study abroad or exchange student, you'll need to have completed your accommodation application by Wednesday 31 December 2025.

And heads up – popular options fill up quickly, so make sure you get in early.

Missed the closing date for the first year guarantee but still want to live on campus in 2026?

You can still apply for accommodation!

We have rooms available on most of our campuses. Apply now via the Accommodation Portal.

2

Safe and supported

Living on campus is a home away from home. A place where you'll be safe and supported.

  • Each dorm or block has its own Residential Leaders. These second or third year students all understand how new students feel and are ready to help. Whether it's to do with your studies, the residences or you just need to chat. And they'll know all the Charles Sturt support services that are available to you.
  • Your room will be like your castle – only you will have the door card or key for entry.
  • Studying late at night or on the weekends? You can request an escort from the security team. They chaperone you to your room or to the library or to your car. Let the security team know where you'll be and they can organise regular walk-bys.
  • Lock yourself out? They'll let you back into your room.
  • Our security teams are student-focused and regularly patrolled all campuses – 24/7. Blue emergency phones, which automatically connect to the security team, are also located around all campuses.
3

All-inclusive costs

Living on campus is made easy. There’s no bond required, and rooms are fully furnished. The weekly fee includes:

  • wi-fi
  • cleaning
  • dedicated study spaces
  • fully furnished living areas and common spaces
  • washing machines and dryers
  • parking
  • maintenance
  • utilities (electricity and water)
  • 24/7 security.

We can also take the stress out of cooking, with food outlets and meal plans available on most campuses.

Live where you learn

At Charles Sturt, we want you to have the uni experience you dream of. That’s why our campuses have everything you need to get your study done and have a fab social life. You’re sure to find your home away from home.

  • Albury-Wodonga
  • Bathurst
