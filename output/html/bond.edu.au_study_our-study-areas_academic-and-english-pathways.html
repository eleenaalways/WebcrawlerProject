Academic and English pathways

For the determined

Overview

Hone your academic skills before commencing an undergraduate degree with one of our English or pathway programs, designed to prepare you for the university landscape. Here at Bond, we make it our mission to create a supportive, welcoming environment for all students regardless of their country of origin, level of education, or previous academic experience which is why we offer a number of preparatory options that are fundamental in developing your knowledge ready for a full degree.  

Delivered by Bond University College, our programs include English language courses for international students wishing to commence a full degree at Bond, as well as a range of academic support options for both domestic and international students that will enforce the core academic competencies needed to excel in further study.

Key features

  • Study at the university with Australias best student-to-teacher ratio of 11:1 (Good Universities Guide 2025)

  • Learn on the main Bond University campus and be part of the Bond community from day one of your program

  • Receive personalised assistance from the Academic Skills Centre, our free on-campus resource designed to help students with English, essay writing, resume building, maths and more

  • Access our full range of support services, designed to create an unforgettable university experience for all students

  • Transition seamlessly into a diploma or full undergraduate degree on completion of the necessary pathway program

For those ready to take the first step towards a brilliant career in whichever path they choose.
