Admission pathways

Find the right pathway for you

There are a number of ways you can gain entry into UNSW and study your dream degree:

Portfolio Entry Early Conditional Offer Scheme:Demonstrate your passion and talent alongside your ATAR by submitting a portfolio. Successful applicants will receive an early conditional offer with an adjusted ATAR entry requirement for a specific degree.

Gateway Admission Pathway: Gain early entry to UNSW or a significantly lower ATAR entry requirement for a range of degrees. To be eligible, you must attend a Gateway schoolorlive in a low-socioeconomic area based on IRSAD and IEO indexes of SEIFA criteria.

UNSW College Diploma:Complete the first year of a bachelor's degree in 12 months and advance directly to the second year with lower ATAR requirements. With options in Business, Engineering, Computer Science, Media and Communications, or Science, and access to UNSWs facilities, over 90% of graduates successfully progress to UNSW.
