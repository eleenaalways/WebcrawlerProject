How to apply in three steps

  • Kick start your journey with us by understanding what path is right for you.

    Admission pathways
    Boost your chances of admission or find the support you need through our range of pathways.

    Degree finder
    Find your dream degree, where you'll become one of Australia's most employable graduates.

    Entry requirements
    Understand accepted qualifications, adjustment factors and assumed knowledge.

  • Double-check all UAC key dates, including on-time application closing dates. Late applications may be accepted but will incur a higher processing fee, so its best to get in early.

    UNSW's main offer round is December Round 2, with the majority of offers made in this round and January Round 1.
    Start your application

  • Keep your UAC preferences updated. As part of your application, you can submit five degree preferences from multiple universities. The order that you preference your courses will influence which degree offer you could get per offer round.You can revisit and change your preferences at any time before the relevant offer rounds.

    Place your dream UNSW degree as your highest eligible preference to be considered for an offer.

    For more information, visit our guide to UAC preferences.

I've submitted my application. What's next?

  • You can track your application or update your preferences online with UAC, by logging into yourUAC account.

    You'll only receive one offer per UAC offer round so make sure your preferences are in the best shape possible. Keep the degree you really want to study as your top preference to be able to receive an offer. You can update your preferences as often as you like before each offer round.

  • Received an offer from us? Congratulations! Your offer means you've been accepted to study at UNSW.

    Most offers to study are made in the UAC December Round 2 and January Round 1 offer rounds. UAC will contact you via email letting you know which degree you have an offer to study. Make sure to check the offer acceptance deadlinesto ensure you secure your spot in time.

    If you haven't received an offer or for the degree you want, you can find out what to do in each situation in UAC's guide.

  • Accept your place by clicking the link in your offer email orgoing toAccept Online.

    It's best to accept your place as early as possible.Each degree at UNSW has limited placestomaximisethequality of teaching and learning, and they can fill up quickly.

  • Here comes the exciting part you get to pick your classes andbeginpreparing for the start of term.Once enrolment for your degree is available, you can enrol in your degree and courses onlineatmyUNSW.

    If you have any questions, our friendlyteamat theNucleus Student Hubcan help you withenrolment, finding your way around campusand getting your student ID card.
